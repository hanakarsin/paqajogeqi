How to eat ginger for weight loss

A 200 ml cup of ginger tea contains hardly 20 calories. It also can be taken with some snacks between main meals which helps to curb the appetite.

Here’s a recipe to make ginger tea for weight loss:

Ingredients:

  • Ginger root or ginger powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Water - 200 ml
  • Honey
  • A slice of lemon

Preparation:

To prepare ginger tea, take fresh, grated roots or dried ginger powder. If you want to prepare tea from fresh root, take a 2-inch long piece, peel it and grate. First, boil the water. Lower the flame, add ginger to boiled water and simmer for a few minutes. Then, strain and add honey and lemon if desired. Remember that if you add honey to make the tea sweat or for a better taste, use only warm water as all the useful vitamins and nutrients included in honey can be destroyed by heat. Alternatively, if the ginger tea is drunk hot, it will bring better benefits for weight loss but, in this case, ginger tea is not advised to be flavored with honey.

The possible side effects of ginger tea

Ginger tea might cause some undesirable side effects if one consumes excessive amounts of this weight loss tea. It is recommended to use no more than 4 grams of dried or grained ginger a day. Studies show that ginger consumed excessively can lead to heartburns and upset stomach. Those who are taking medicine to improve digestive or blood systems should stay away from using this herb both as a tea and spice as it is known to ruin the effect of such medications.

The best time to drink ginger tea

To make most out of ginger tea drink it as early in the morning as you can, preferably, a half an hour right before breakfast, on an empty stomach. Drinking ginger tea between main meals will curb the appetite. Right before going to sleep, drink ginger tea again.
