DT Dobie Kenya contacts and locations

March 2024 · 5 minute read

DT Dobie is one of the major Kenyan subsidiaries of the Conception et Fabrication Assisteè par Ordinateur (CFAO Automotive). CFAO Automotive is a large network that operates in both Africa and French overseas territories. It is the company that owns DT Dobie Kenya. The company started out as a Mercedes-Benz franchise for East Africa. With such a huge customer base, chances are that you are not the only one who wants to talk to DT Dobie. We have listed all DT Dobie Kenya contacts to help you get in touch with the customer care desk fast when you need expert help.

A World War II veteran, Colonel David Dobie, started DT Dobie in 1949. In the year 2000, CFAO Automotive acquired the business, and since then, it has grown and extended its services throughout many parts of African and French overseas territories. The company is the authorized distributor for Mercedes-Benz car, Volkswagen, Mercedes-Benz truck, GWM pick-ups and Hyundai.
