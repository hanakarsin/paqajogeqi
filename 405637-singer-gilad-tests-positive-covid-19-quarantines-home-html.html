Singer Gilad tests positive for COVID-19, quarantines at home

March 2024 Â· 3 minute read

- Popular artiste Gilad has confirmed that he tested positive for COVID-19

- The singer also said that he has notified every person he was in contact with concerning his new development

- His fans and other celebrities sent him encouraging and quick recovery messages

PAY ATTENTION:

Popular artiste Gilad has tested positive for COVID-19 and he will be self-isolating at his home.

The Sema Milele song maker decided to do the test after developing a slight fever and feeling other health issues.

He posted on his social media pages, alerting his fans of the new development and also cautioning his followers to take precautions.

''Hi everyone. After feeling some aches and pains and developing a slight fever today I tested positive for COVID-19. I have alerted those I have been in contact with in line with the required protocols. I will be quarantining at home until this passes God willing and will be back at it before you can say 'Corona'. Stay safe and please take the instructions on this seriously as it is unfortunately very real,'' he posted on Facebook.
