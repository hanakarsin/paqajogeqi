Charly Boy Blasts Asari Dokubo As Local Vigilantes Overpower His 'Terrorist' Gangs

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Veteran Nigerian entertainer, Charles Oputa, popularly known as Charly Boy, has slammed former Niger Delta militant leader, Asari Dokubo, as local vigilantes allegedly overpower his Islamic militia in Anambra State.

Naija News recalls that Dokubo had warned enemies of President Tinubu to stay away from him because anyone who plans his downfall has failed already.

Dokubo gave the warning while addressing his gang members of about 300 militants after his return from a pilgrimage to Mecca.

In a viral video, the militant leader said he and his comrades are working for the president, and anyone seeking his downfall will be smashed.

Taking to his Instagram page on Saturday, Charly Boy shared a cartoon of Dokubo vowing to protect President Tinubu.

The singer cum political activist described the ex-militant as an over-blotted obsessed toad who loves taking rubbish.

He alleged that Anambra youths resisted and captured the fake Biafrans enforcing sit-at-home across the southeast states.

Charly Boy added that Buhari ruled the country with the help of Boko Haram and Myetti Allah, and Tinubu prefers to rule with the help of terrorist groups and unknown gunmen.

He wrote: “Humm, see somebori forming militant, an over-blotted obsessed toad who loves talking shit and spraying his saliva all over d place.

“Asari, the terrorist Dokubo’s terrorists gang where recently overpowered by ordinary local vigilantes in Ogbaru Anambra. The youth resisted and captured the fake Biafrans enforcing ‘Sit @ Home’. Imo, Anambra, Enugu and Ebonyi what are ya all waiting for?

“Buhari ruled Nigeria with the help of Boko Haram and Mayetti Allah. Emilokan preferred to rule us with the help of the Niger Delta Volatile terrorist group and unknown gunmen.

“U are aware dat most of killings going on in the SE are nonindigenes and not Igbos. We Must Stop Dis Criminal Enterprise.

“We plenty Pass Dem. A New Nigeria Is Possible”
