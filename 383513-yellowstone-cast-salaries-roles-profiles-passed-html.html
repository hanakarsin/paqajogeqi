Frequently asked questions about Yellowstone cast

If you're a fan of the show, you probably have a plethora of questions about the cast and the show. Here are some of the most frequently asked questions about the Yellowstone cast.

Is Kevin Costner's son in Yellowstone?

Kevin's son, Joe, is also an actor. Most of the fans are interested in knowing where Costner's son has featured in the Yellowstone series. However, his real-life son has not landed a lead or cameo role in the series.

Who plays as the Governor on Yellowstone?

Wendy Moniz plays the role of Governor Lynelle Perry on Yellowstone. In the series, we can see things heating up between her and John Dutton; a role played by Kevin Costner.

Is Yellowstone filmed on Kevin Costner's ranch?

The filming of Yellowstone is not done on Kevin Costner's ranch. It is done Chief Joseph's ranch in Darby, Montana. The mansion, which is 5,000 square feet, is over 100 years old. It was built in 1917 by William Ford and his family. The main living room of the Chief Joseph Ranch lodge was decorated and used as its shooting location.

Which son died on Yellowstone?

Lee Dutton, the first son and heir of the Dutton's ranch, is the one who died on Yellowstone. He died in the first episode of the first series during a major shootout over a dispute about a cattle raid. Kayce tried to save him but he succumbed to his wounds and was buried at the family gravesite.

Is Yellowstone based on a true story?

Unfortunately, the show is not based on a true story, but it has an interesting and a major storyline. It is not based on a true-life event that probably occurred years ago, but it speaks for countless landowners who are facing the threat of eviction from their land. These are real-life aspects.
