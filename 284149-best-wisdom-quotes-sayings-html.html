Best wisdom quotes and sayings

Wisdom quotes offer two things: insight and poetry. Their beauty is revealed by their universality meanings since everyone relates them in some way and on some level. Basically, wisdom quotes are inspirational words that uplift and remind us of life lessons that are essential in building our character. Probably, these are the most profound words you will ever hear.

Majority of the wisdom quotes are derived from the elderly in the society. Therefore, one can argue that age and wisdom quotes are complimentary. We are all here to live but our individual experiences in life differ. This is the reason why we have so many wisdom quotes to try to suit in all our different experiences. Broadly, wisdom quotes, are like pithy sayings stating a precaution, general truth, motivating or even giving advice. In addition, wise sayings are used in different languages hence the targeted audience is expansive. Ever thought of some of the most animating wise quotes and sayings? Lost time is never found

This is one of the most popular and great quotes of wisdom. It was devised by Benjamin Franklin, a polymath, who is one of the founding fathers of the United States of America. This adage is open and largely gives advice on the importance of keeping time. Most of the times, our daily plans are normally behind our planned schedule. This wisdom quote explains the importance of keeping time in all daily activities. Once a moment is gone, it’s burned up eternally. It can never be regained.

No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted.

This is another inspiring wisdom quote. Kindness is the act of being friendly, generous and considerate to others. The moral of this quote reveals that no one, however small or inferior, can be of some use. Nobody should be despised for their lowly position in life. Kindness cost very little but is of high value when returned. This quote hence gives a lesson on the importance of kindness in the society.

Our dead are never dead to us, until we have forgotten them.

This is one of George Eliot’s wisdom emotional quotes. Although the death of loved ones is of a greatest loss, this wise quote gives hope to mourners. Death brings with it depression, loneliness, and above all pain. Eliot’s aim was to induce smile and help gather strength from such distress. The saying passes the information of hope during grief moments. Therefore, the love for the death becomes stronger than the death itself.

Respecting someone indicates the quality of your personality

Originally written by Mohammad Rishad, it can be classified under wisdom discipline quotes. The architect of this quote is trying to insist on the importance of dignity in the society. Respect is a way of passing a message and builds a strong companionship. Further, respect reflects the value for one another in the society. Anyone’s suggestions and comments are equal and essential to receiving audience. To that end, respect is a mutual process cannot, therefore, be demanded.

So even though we face the difficulties of today and tomorrow, I still have a dream.

This quote is one of Martin Luther’s top wisdom quotes. Here, the leader was prophesying about his country’s future. What Martin Luther implies is that even though we may face difficulties today towards achieving our goals, our hope for the future is still bright. He gave an example of the state of Mississippi that was sweltering with injustice. He foretold that it will become an oasis of freedom and justice in the future.

Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet.

Patience is the act of having composure when doing any activity. It is the state of perseverance that comes when expecting results. This is the best wisdom quotes to induce encouragement. However, being patient is tough. Therefore, the author of this saying tries to give hope for enduring hard times during the fight for success. Interestingly, the results of hard work become a source of enjoyment owing to the tough path to achieve it.
