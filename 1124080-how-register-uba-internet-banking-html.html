UBA internet banking registration process in 2024: how is it done?

February 2024 · 5 minute read

The United Bank for Africa (UBA) is one of the major banks in Nigeria. The financial institution has evolved and improved its services as technology evolves. UBA offers clients safe, convenient, affordable financial services through Internet banking. UBA Internet banking service enables customers to transact online without going to the bank. Below is a simple guide to the UBA Internet banking registration process.

UBA Nigeria has its headquarters in Lagos. The bank introduced Internet banking services to enable customers to enjoy and personalize baking services. UBA's Internet banking service is secure and saves time, as financial transactions can be done anywhere. Learn more about the UBA online banking registration process.

UBA Internet banking registration

What is UBA Internet banking? This is a digital platform where customers can remotely carry out all banking transactions. All you need is your account details and Internet connectivity. UBA has introduced Internet banking services to help customers access financial services on the go.

How do you register for UBA Internet banking?

To enjoy the UBA Internet banking service, you will need to register. The registration process is free and easy. Below is a step-by-step guide for UBA online banking registration process.

  • Go to the UBA Internet banking page.
  • Click on the red box written "UBA Internet Banking" on the left side of your page.
  • Select the "Personal" or "Business" icon.
  • You will be directed to a new page, where you will find another box.
  • Select "Instant Self-Registration" to start registering.
  • Select your user type. If you are an individual, select "Retail" and click "Continue".
  • Fill out the form by entering your account number.
  • Fill in your card details. This includes the first six, last four digits and the debit card PIN and click "Continue".
  • Create a new password and repeat to confirm it.
  • Complete the registration process.
  • You can now log in to your UBA account online.

    UBA corporate Internet banking

    UBA has a different online portal for its corporate clients. Corporate organizations can access the UBA Business Direct, an online portal for financial services. This portal covers all banking needs for businesses and big organizations. To register for the UBA Business Direct, follow these simple steps.

  • Go to the UBA website.
  • Select "Corporate".
  • Visit the "Banking Instant Self-Registration" site.
  • Register by filling in the account details.
  • Create a new password and reenter it to confirm it.
  • Use your UBA Internet banking login to access your UBA Business Direct account to transact.

    UBA mobile banking registration

    Mobile banking has become common as most bank account holders have mobile phones. UBA has enabled mobile banking, known UMobile, for its customers. To access this service, users need to register at the bank. Below are the steps to register for UBA mobile banking.

  • Visit any UBA bank branch near you.
  • Head customer care and request to be enrolled for UBA mobile banking services.
  • You will get a mobile banking application form from the agent.
  • Fill in the form with your details and hand over your identity documents.
  • Once registered, you will get a confirmation message on the phone number you used to register the account.
  • You can download the UBA mobile app on your phone and start transacting.
