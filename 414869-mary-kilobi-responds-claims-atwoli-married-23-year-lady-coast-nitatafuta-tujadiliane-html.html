Mary Kilobi Responds to Claims Atwoli Married 23-Year-Old Lady in Coast: &quot;Nitamtafuta Tujadiliane&quot;

April 2024 · 3 minute read

- KTN presenter Mary Kilobi has hilariously responded to rumours that her husband Francis Atwoli married another woman last weekend

- Mary and Atwoli were in Watamu for his birthday when the rumour surfaced and spread like wildfire

- Mary's message comes barely 24 hours after Atwoli dismissed the same rumours, saying there is no problem marrying another woman

Francis Atwoli's wife, Mary Kilobi, has finally broken her silence following a rumour that her husband secretly married a 23-year-old woman identified as Jamila Mbugua. The rumour spread like wildfire over the weekend, leaving tongues wagging.

This forced Atwoli to address the rumour, and he denied marrying another woman. The Central Organisation of Trade Unions (COTU) boss and Mary flew to Watamu, Kilifi county, to celebrate his 71st birthday past weekend.

Atwoli and Mary enjoyed their stay in the coastal city. On Sunday, they went on a yacht cruise with one of his sons, and Devolution Cabinet Secretary Eugene Wamalwa.

Mysterious wife

Mary shared a post making fun of Jamila, the lady who many claimed got married to Atwoli last weekend. The KTN presenter jokingly said she would like to meet her to discuss the way forward.

"Huyu dem wa 23 years old msomali au sijui Jamila Mbugua si ametesa jameni? Watu hawalali! Mkimuona salimia yeye sana. Nitamtafuta tujadiliane (This 23-year-old lady Somalian Jamila Mbugua has really trended. People are not sleeping! If you see her say hi to her. I will look for her we discuss)," Mary wrote.

Mary's followers, among them State House spokesperson Kanze Dena reacted to her post, and below are the reactions:

No fourth wife

Atwoli dismissed the rumours going round, saying there is no problem marrying another woman.

He said if he has to, then he would do it publicly.

"That is a lazy rumour from some idle Kenyan. I was at the coast with my wife Mary (Kilobi Atwoli). In any case, there is no problem in marrying but if I have to do that I do it publicly not hiding from anyone," Atwoli exclusively told TUKO.co.ke.
