Gamer who has risen to fame for his Jay3 Twitch channel. His Overwatch gaming streams and playthroughs have helped him gain more than 500,000 followers. He was a professional player for Renegades before becoming a streamer.  

Before Fame

He played Team Fortress 2 competitively before shifting his focus to Overwatch. He made his Overwatch tournament debut in July 2016 at the ESL Overwatch Atlantic Showdown Gamescom NA Qualifier #1. 

Trivia

He won the DreamHack Montreal 2018 Overwatch tournament playing for Mirage Sport Electronique. He has also played Overwatch for Florida Mayhem as a content creator on Twitch. 

Family Life

His brother and father are both named Paul. His girlfriend's name is Audrey. He has a dog named Mila. 

Associated With

He has gamed alongside Samito for the Florida Mayhem's Overwatch content creator squad.
